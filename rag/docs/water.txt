OBTAINING POTABLE WATER IN EMERGENCIES

Purification methods:
1. Boil water for at least 1 minute (3 minutes above 2000m altitude)
2. Chlorine or iodine tablets: follow manufacturer's instructions
3. Activated carbon filters: remove particles but not viruses
4. Solar distillation: transparent container, plastic on top, collector container below

Urban water sources during a blackout:
- Domestic water heaters: contain 150-300 liters
- Toilet cisterns/tanks: clean water (not the water in the bowl)
- Pipe water: before they run dry
